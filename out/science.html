The Science Behind Orin

Understanding ADHD and executive dysfunction through research and real experiences

The Rising Need

According to Yale Medicine, ADHD diagnoses in adults have been rising significantly:

61% increase in adults ages 30-44 (January 2021 - October 2024)
64% increase in adults ages 45-64 (January 2021 - October 2024)

This rising trend highlights a critical need for tools designed specifically for adult executive dysfunction.

Research from Harvard Health suggests traditional ADHD management strategies:
